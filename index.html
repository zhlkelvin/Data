Job growth and disparity between men and women in Canada

With the disparity of employment between the sexes, do recessions cause the disparity to widen or recess? Men and women on a larger scale pursue different fields of work whethber by choice or by societal forces. How do those differences reflected during times of recession?

The objective is to investigate the trends of employment between the two sexes in Canada over the course of the past 40 years. By understanding how employment is affected by the macro market sentiments we can use this data to support decision making for employers who want maintain and equitable workspace.
